EduQuest — Adaptive Learning Through Gameplay

A gamified learning platform where AI generates personalised quiz questions based on each student's knowledge gaps, tracks progress over sessions, and includes an anti-cheating system that detects suspicious patterns without punishing honest mistakes. Built for students aged 12–22, designed to make studying feel less like a chore and more like a challenge worth winning.
